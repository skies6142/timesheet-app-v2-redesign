What's in it

Everything your crew needs in one place

Replaces the group chat, the spreadsheet, and the pile of paper dockets on the dash.

Job Calendar
Schedule jobs on a visual calendar. Add client name, number, location and job notes. See the whole week at a glance and tap a day to see what's on.
Crew Management
Assign workers to jobs and manage roles — owner, admin, employee, subcontractor. Share your 6-digit invite code and crew join in seconds.
Notes & Checklists
Shared notes for your team. Make checklists, drag items to reorder them, and control who can view or edit each note. Everything syncs live.
Invoice Submissions
Workers submit invoice reports from their phone. Owners approve, reject or mark as paid — no more chasing paperwork at the end of the week.
Timesheets
Built-in timer for clocking on and off. Auto-calculates hours and pay. Runs in the background with notification controls so nothing gets missed.
Job Check-ins
Workers check in and out at each job. You see live status — who's on site, who's wrapped up, and who hasn't clocked on yet.
How it works

Up and running in a few minutes

No training session, no setup fees. Sign up, add a job, share your invite code.

1
Create your organisation
Sign up, name your business, and you're in. Share your 6-digit invite code with your crew — they're on in seconds.
2
Add jobs & assign crew
Schedule jobs on the calendar with client details and location. Tap to assign whoever's working that day.
3
Track hours & invoices
Workers clock on, track hours, and submit invoices when done. You approve and keep everyone across the work.
Built for everyone on the job

Owners run it. The whole crew uses it.

Docket is designed for the person running the show — but every role gets exactly what they need and nothing they don't.

Business Owner / Admin
You're in control
Full access to every feature. Schedule jobs, manage your crew, and keep tabs on everything from one place. Admins get the same access so you're not the only one holding it all together.
  • Create and manage all jobs
  • Approve or reject invoice submissions
  • View timesheet reports by worker or job
  • Set note visibility and edit permissions
  • Manage crew roles and access
  • See live check-in status across all sites
Employee / Subcontractor
Just turn up and tap
See your jobs for the day, clock on, track your hours, and submit your invoice when the work is done. No calls, no paperwork, no guessing what's on.
  • See your assigned jobs for the week
  • Check in and out at the job site
  • Built-in timer with notification controls
  • Submit invoice reports straight from the app
  • Access shared notes and checklists
  • Works on iPhone and Android as a PWA
